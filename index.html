Who We Are

An NGO located in Ahemdabad, Gujarat in India that focuses on eduction and servicing the underpriviledged within the city. With the guidence of Mahatma Gandhi's philosophy, we hope to transform underpriveldged communities. Our staff and volunteers from all around the world come together to "Love All, Serve All".

Our Inspiration

Mahatma Gandhi's words, "Love All, Serve All" are our main inspiration. We hope to continue using his message to do good within our community and spread his philosophy.
